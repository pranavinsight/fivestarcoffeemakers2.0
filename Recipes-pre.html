Classic Iced Coffee

Ingredients:

  1. Freshly brewed coffee (strong)
  2. Ice cubes
  3. Milk or cream
  4. Sweetener (sugar, syrup, etc.) - optional

Instructions:

Brew a strong pot of coffee or espresso and let it cool to room temperature. Fill a glass with ice cubes. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice cubes, leaving room for milk or cream. Add milk or cream to your desired taste. Sweeten with sugar or syrup if desired. Stir well and enjoy your refreshing iced coffee!

2. Café Mocha

Ingredients:

1 shot of espresso (or strong brewed coffee) 1 tablespoon cocoa powder 1 tablespoon sugar 1 cup milk Whipped cream (optional) Chocolate syrup (optional) Instructions: Brew a shot of espresso or make strong coffee. In a small saucepan, combine the cocoa powder, sugar, and a splash of milk to form a paste. Gradually add the rest of the milk and heat over medium heat, whisking constantly until hot and frothy. Pour the espresso into a mug, then add the hot chocolate mixture. Stir well to combine. Top with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup if desired. Enjoy your homemade café mocha! 3. Vanilla Iced Coffee Ingredients: Freshly brewed coffee (chilled) Ice cubes 1-2 tablespoons vanilla syrup Milk or cream Instructions: Brew a pot of coffee and let it cool in the refrigerator. Fill a glass with ice cubes. Pour the chilled coffee over the ice cubes. Add vanilla syrup to taste. Add milk or cream to your liking. Stir well and savor the delightful vanilla flavor in your iced coffee! 4. Cinnamon Dolce Latte Ingredients: 1 shot of espresso (or strong brewed coffee) 1 tablespoon cinnamon dolce syrup (or cinnamon and brown sugar mixture) 1 cup steamed milk Ground cinnamon for garnish Instructions: Brew a shot of espresso or prepare strong coffee. In a mug, combine the espresso and cinnamon dolce syrup. Steam or heat the milk until frothy. Pour the steamed milk over the coffee mixture. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon for extra flavor. Enjoy the warm and comforting cinnamon dolce latte! 5. Affogato Ingredients: 1-2 scoops of vanilla ice cream or gelato 1 shot of hot espresso Instructions: Place the scoops of vanilla ice cream or gelato in a dessert bowl or glass. Pour a shot of hot espresso over the ice cream. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of hot espresso and cold ice cream.
